\chapter*{Critical Report}

This edition bases upon Zelenka’s autograph, which has been digitized by the Staatsbibliothek zu Berlin. The digital version of the manuscript is available at \url{http://resolver.staatsbibliothek-berlin.de/SBB0001A60700000000} (siglum Mus.ms.autogr. Zelenka, J. D. 1 M; see also RISM ID 464141552).

In general, this edition closely follows the manuscript. Any changes that were introduced by the editor are indicated by italic type (lyrics, dynamics and directions), parentheses (expressive marks and bass figures) or dashes (slurs and ties). Accidentals are used according to modern conventions. Asterisks denote changes that are clarified in the detailed remarks below.\footnote{Abbreviations: A, alto; B, bass; b, basses; Ms, manuscript; org, organ; r, rest; S, soprano; T, tenor.}
